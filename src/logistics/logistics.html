Transportation

To the event

From the airport to Kiana Lodge:

The Seattle-Tacoma International Airport is approximately 1.5 hours away from Kiana Lodge.

If flying in day-of, we recommend renting a car.

From the airport to Seattle:

Alternatively, we recommend flying in early and spending time in Seattle.

A taxi to downtown Seattle is typically $40. Rideshares such as Lyft and Uber tend to run cheaper. Without traffic, it is about a 20 minute drive.

The Sound Transit Link light rail arrives every 6-15 minutes and takes about 40 minutes. One way fare ranges from $2.25 to $3.25.

The Shuttle Express Downtown Airporter offers shuttle service starting at $18 per person to downtown Seattle hotels.

Check out visitseattle.org for more options and details.

There are many lodging options in Seattle. Consider renting an Airbnb as a hotel alternative.

Getting from Seattle to Kiana Lodge:

While you can drive around to the peninsula (~1.5 hours), the preferred method is to take the Seattle-Bainbridge ferry.

‚ö†Ô∏è Due to the holiday weekend, ferries may have long wait times for cars.

You can drive a car on the ferry for about $18 one-way. Walk-on passengers are $8.35 each; fares for walk-ons are only collected from Seattle to Bainbridge and the return trip is free. There is no designated parking near the ferry terminal, so if you choose to walk on you may want to find alternative methods for getting to the ferry terminal.

If taking the light rail (either from the airport or downtown) it is a 10 minute walk to the ferry terminal from the Pioneer Square Station.

From the Bainbridge Ferry Terminal to Kiana Lodge:

You can drive, Uber, taxi, or bus to Poulsbo and/or the Clearwater Hotel.

There is also a free courtesy shuttle from the ferry to the Clearwater if you are a club card member. If you do not have a club card you can tell the driver that you will get a club card upon your arrival.

From the Clearwater Hotel to Kiana Lodge:

There will be a complimentary shuttle from the hotel to Kiana Lodge for the wedding. Shuttles to the event leave at 4:50pm.
